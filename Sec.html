Other Rules

  1. Always update windows and other software
  2. Change any passwords once they are know
  3. do not repeat passwords and usernames
  4. Never share passwords with these accounts Banking, Email, Credit accounts, Microsoft, and Password managers
  5. each one of the should be unique and not repeat
